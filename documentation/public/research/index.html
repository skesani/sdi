A Bio-Inspired Cybersecurity Framework for Autonomous Threat Detection and Self-Healing Microservices

Abstract#

This paper presents Synthetic Digital Immunity (SDI), an AI-driven cybersecurity architecture inspired by biological immune systems. SDI provides autonomous threat detection, isolation, and self-healing capabilities for microservices architectures.
